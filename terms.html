Who We Are

Tilth Automation (“Tilth,” “we,” “us”) provides workflow analysis, automation design, and implementation services to freelancers, virtual assistants, bookkeepers, and small service businesses. Services are delivered by human practitioners supported by AI tooling.

Refund Policy

We believe in our work. If we fail to deliver, you get your money back. No games.

Short version: Full refund if we fail to deliver on time or the work doesn’t match what was agreed. 50% refund if you cancel after work has begun.

Full Refund — You are entitled to a full refund if:

  • We do not deliver within the stated timeline (from completed questionnaire), unless delay was caused by your unresponsiveness
  • The deliverable does not materially match what was described and agreed upon in writing
  • We are unable to begin work due to factors within our control

Partial Refund — A 50% refund applies if:

  • You cancel after work has begun but before delivery
  • The deliverable partially meets the agreed scope and we cannot resolve the gap within one revision cycle

No Refund — Refunds are not available if:

  • You chose not to implement the recommendations (Tier 1) or use the deliverable (Tiers 2/3)
  • The automation worked as designed but your underlying process changed
  • Delays were caused by your failure to respond, provide access, or complete the onboarding questionnaire within the stated windows
  • You approved the scope document and the delivered work matches that scope

How to Request a Refund

Email us at admin@tilth.dev within 14 days of delivery (or expected delivery date, if we are late). Describe the gap between what was promised and what was delivered. We will respond within 2 business days. If we owe you a refund, it is processed within 5 business days.
